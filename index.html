BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index using height and weight.

What Is BMI and Why It Matters

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple and widely used method to understand whether your body weight is healthy for your height. It uses your height and weight to calculate a number that helps classify you as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. BMI is commonly used by health professionals as a quick screening tool to identify potential weight-related health risks.

How This BMI Calculator Works

This BMI calculator allows you to calculate your Body Mass Index by entering your age, gender, height, and weight. You can choose different measurement units such as kilograms or pounds for weight and centimeters, inches, or feet for height. The calculator automatically converts all values and provides an accurate BMI result instantly.

BMI Categories Explained

After calculating your BMI, the result falls into one of the standard BMI categories. These categories are used worldwide as general guidelines:

  • Underweight: BMI below 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or above

Age and Gender Considerations

BMI interpretation can vary depending on age and gender. For adults, BMI is a useful general indicator of healthy weight. However, for children and teenagers under 18, BMI depends on age- and gender-specific percentiles. That is why this calculator provides a warning message for users below 18 years of age.

Different Units Supported

To make the calculator convenient for users around the world, this BMI calculator supports multiple measurement systems. You can calculate BMI using kilograms or pounds for weight and centimeters, inches, or feet for height. Dedicated pages are also available for specific unit combinations to make calculation even easier.

Is BMI Always Accurate?

BMI is a helpful screening tool, but it does not directly measure body fat, muscle mass, or overall fitness. Athletes or individuals with high muscle mass may have a higher BMI even if they are healthy. Therefore, BMI should be used as a general guideline rather than a medical diagnosis.

Who Can Use This BMI Calculator

This BMI calculator is designed for adults who want a quick and easy way to check their weight status. It is suitable for both men and women and supports different unit preferences. However, pregnant individuals and people with specific medical conditions should consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

Important Disclaimer

The information provided by this BMI calculator is for educational and informational purposes only. It should not be considered medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for personalized health guidance and recommendations.
